About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Engineering at North Carolina State University (NCSU), working under the supervision of Dr. Do Young Eun. My research interests lie at the intersection of applied probability and machine learning, with a specific focus on Markov Chain Monte Carlo (MCMC), Distributed Optimization, and Generative AI.

Prior to joining NCSU, I earned my M.S. in Computer Engineering from NYU Tandon School of Engineering. I am passionate about leveraging theoretical insights to build scalable AI systems and solve complex network challenges.
